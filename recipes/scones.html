Scones

Delicious and easy savoury scones, fluffy inside, well risen and with plenty of cheese!

Ingredients

  • 100g white self raising flour
  • 100g brown self raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 50g butter
  • 50g grated mature cheddar
  • Â½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 egg
  • 60 ml milk

Steps

  1. Mix together the flours, baking powder and salt.

  2. Rub in the butter.
  3. Stir in the grated cheese and herbs.
  4. Beat together the egg and milk.
  5. Add enough liquid, to make a soft dough.
  6. Knead gently and stop as soon as it is smooth.
  7. Roll out to 2 cm thick.
  8. Cut into rounds and place on greased baking tray.
  9. Brush with the egg and milk.
  10. Bake in a hot oven, 200 C for 10 to 15 minutes.
  11. Cool on a wire rack.
