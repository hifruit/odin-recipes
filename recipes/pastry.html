Delicious Pastry

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • pinch salt
  • 50g trex (or other white fat)
  • 50g margarine
  • 3 tablespoons cold water (approximately)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and salt together.
  2. Rub in the fat to resemble breadcrumbs.

  3. Mix in enough cold water to form a stiff paste.
    (Do this with a metal spoon or knife initially, to avoid
    warming the pastry with your hands.)

  4. Wrap the dough in cling film and refrigerate for at
    least 30 minutes before using.
